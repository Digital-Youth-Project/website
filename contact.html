Affiliation

The Digital Youth Project is based at the Department of Psychology, Faculty of Social and Behavioural Sciences, University of Amsterdam. The project is affiliated with the Research Priority Area Youth Digitality at UvA.

Looking for collaborators

We actively seek partnerships with organisations that work with or care about young people. Collaboration can take different forms, from participating in a study to co-designing research, sharing data, or co-authoring publications.

🏫 Secondary schools: for focus groups, surveys, or longitudinal data collection with adolescent populations
🏥 Clinics & mental health services: to reach clinical samples and translate findings into practice
🔬 Research groups: for shared data analysis, consortium building, or methodological exchange
🌐 Policy & public bodies: to translate research findings into evidence-based guidance for youth and digital media
