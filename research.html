Our work

Research

We study what happens when young people's digital lives and their mental health collide. Some adolescents seem unaffected by heavy social media or gaming use; others develop real difficulties — and we want to understand why. Our research draws on multiple approaches, from in-depth conversations with teenagers to large-scale longitudinal data to controlled lab studies, to get at questions that no single method can answer alone.
